Privacy Policy for Travel GPT

Effective date: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Travel GPT (“the Service”) handles information when users interact with its travel-search and travel-planning features.

The Service may process information that a user voluntarily enters, including departure and destination locations, travel dates, number of travelers, preferred cabin class, budget, accommodation preferences, and other travel-related requests.

Users should not submit passwords, payment-card information, passport numbers, government identification numbers, precise home addresses, health information, or other sensitive personal information.

2. How information is used

Information is used only to provide requested travel-related functions, such as searching for flights, comparing travel options, and generating itinerary or preparation suggestions.

3. External service providers

To complete a request, the Service may send relevant search parameters to third-party API providers, such as SerpAPI or another travel-search provider configured for this GPT. These providers process information under their own privacy policies and terms.

Only information reasonably necessary to perform the requested search should be transmitted. The Service does not intentionally send a user's full ChatGPT conversation when a smaller set of travel-search parameters is sufficient.

The operator of this GPT does not maintain a separate user database and does not intentionally store personal information received through GPT Actions, except where temporary technical logs may be created by an external API or hosting provider. The operator does not sell or rent users’ personal information. Information may be shared with an external API provider only as needed to perform a user-requested function, or where disclosure is required by law.

This Service operates within ChatGPT. Information submitted in ChatGPT is also subject to OpenAI’s applicable terms, privacy policy, and user settings. This Privacy Policy covers only the operator’s handling of data through this GPT and its configured Actions.

Reasonable efforts are made to limit data transmission to what is necessary for each request. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Children’s privacy
